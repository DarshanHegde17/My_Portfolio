CERTIFICATE IMAGES FOLDER
=========================

Place your certificate images in this folder with the following names:
- cert1.jpg (or .png)
- cert2.jpg (or .png)
- cert3.jpg (or .png)
- cert4.jpg (or .png)
- cert5.jpg (or .png)
- cert6.jpg (or .png)

Recommended image size: 400x300 pixels or similar aspect ratio (4:3)

If you don't have certificate images yet, you can:
1. Take screenshots of your certificates
2. Download certificate images from the issuing platforms
3. Use placeholder images temporarily

The images will be displayed in the Certificates section of your portfolio.
